Title: Campaign & Analytics Overview

1. Campaign Setup
Users can create campaigns under Campaign Manager.
Required fields:
- Budget
- Target audience
- Duration

2. Analytics Dashboard
Metrics available:
- CTR
- Conversion Rate
- Impressions
- Cost per Acquisition

3. Data Refresh Frequency
Analytics data refreshes every 15 minutes for Pro & Enterprise plans.
Starter plan refreshes every 2 hours.
