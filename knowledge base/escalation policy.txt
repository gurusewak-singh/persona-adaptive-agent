Title: Support Escalation Policy

Escalation is required when:
- User expresses legal threat
- Sentiment score < -0.7
- Repeated issue reported more than 3 times
- Enterprise customer reports system outage

Escalation Process:
1. Generate issue summary
2. Attach conversation transcript
3. Include detected persona
4. Include sentiment trend
5. Notify Tier-2 Support Team

Priority Levels:
P1 - Critical Business Impact
P2 - Major Feature Impact
P3 - Minor Issue
P4 - Informational Request
