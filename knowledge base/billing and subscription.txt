Title: Billing and Subscription Management

1. Subscription Plans
- Starter Plan: Basic campaign management
- Pro Plan: Advanced analytics & automation
- Enterprise Plan: Dedicated support & SLA

2. Billing Cycle
Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually.
Invoices are generated on the 1st of each billing cycle.

3. Duplicate Charges
If a user is charged twice:
- Verify invoice history
- Confirm whether add-on services were purchased
- Refund requests are processed within 5-7 business days

4. Payment Failures
Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Expired card
- Payment gateway timeout

Users can update payment methods under Billing Settings.
