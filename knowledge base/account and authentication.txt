Title: Account Access & Authentication Guide

1. Password Reset
Users can reset their password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page. 
A reset link is sent to the registered email address and expires in 15 minutes.

If the reset email is not received:
- Check spam folder
- Ensure correct email address was entered
- Verify corporate firewall rules

2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA can be enabled from Account Settings > Security.
Supported methods:
- Email OTP
- Authenticator App (Google Authenticator compatible)

3. Login Failures
Common causes:
- Incorrect credentials
- Expired session token
- Account temporarily locked after 5 failed attempts

Accounts auto-unlock after 30 minutes.
